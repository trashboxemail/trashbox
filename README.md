# Trashbox — Free Temporary Email Service

**[trashbox.email](https://trashbox.email)** — Instant disposable inbox. No signup. No tracking. Auto-deletes in 2 hours.

---

## What is Trashbox?

Trashbox is a free temporary email service that generates a disposable inbox instantly. Use it to receive OTP codes, sign-up verifications, and newsletters without exposing your real email address.

No registration. No personal data collected. All emails and attachments are permanently deleted after 2 hours.

## Features

- **Instant inbox** — no signup, no captcha, ready in seconds
- **Custom aliases** — choose your own email address
- **OTP auto-detection** — verification codes and confirmation links highlighted for one-click copy
- **Attachment support** — download files while your inbox is active
- **20 languages** — English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Turkish, Arabic, Indonesian, Ukrainian, Czech, Swedish, Finnish, Vietnamese
- **Dark mode** — automatic theme switching
- **Mobile-friendly** — fully responsive on any device
- **No ads in your inbox** — clean reading experience
- **Email HTML sanitization** — scripts, trackers, and malicious content stripped server-side

## How It Works

1. **Visit** [trashbox.email](https://trashbox.email) — a random email address is generated instantly
2. **Copy** the address and paste it into any sign-up form or verification field
3. **Receive** emails — messages appear in your inbox within seconds
4. **Done** — after 2 hours, everything is permanently deleted

## Use Cases

- Receive OTP and verification codes without exposing your real email
- Sign up for free trials, downloads, and gated content
- Test email flows during development and QA
- Avoid spam from one-time registrations
- Stay anonymous on forums and marketplaces
